Contemporary Artist - Ding Yi 丁㇠

Ding Yi is a Chinese contemporary artist who was born in 1968 in Shanghai, China. He grew up in during the Cultural Revolution, formally the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, Yi started studying art in middle school and then would further his education in art in Shanghai. However, the art he learned there wasn't systematic. The nonlinear curriculum at the time consisted of making bricks for bomb shelters, drawing propaganda posters, performing in public spaces, and studying design and Chinese ink painting. At Shanghai School of Arts and Crafts Yi studied decorative design between 1980 and 1983 and, later went to major in Chinese ink painting in the art program at Shanghai University from 1986 to 1990.
